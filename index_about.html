Logic Playground

What is Logic Playground?

Logic Playground is an interactive education game website established in 2023 by Ella Raputri. This website is aimed to help young children to learn in a funner way.


What are the features of Logic Playground?

Logic Playground has many interactive games, but most of them are still in development state. Logic Playground can also save your gameplay records if you have a new account. You can sign up or login by clicking the corresponding button in the header.


Can I play the games without having an account?

Yes, you can, but you can't have your records saved in the website.


Lastly, Logic Playground Development Team would like to say thank you for playing! We hope that this online playground can soothe you from the stress of learning and make you fall in love with learning!
